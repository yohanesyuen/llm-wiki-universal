A standing verification rule can become redundant once the underlying system grows a cheaper path, but nothing automatically prompts re-examining it. Periodically re-check whether a standing habit still needs the expensive path it was written for. Prefer fixing the redundancy at the layer that has the information rather than "remember to check less often" on the agent side — which just reintroduces the original staleness risk.
