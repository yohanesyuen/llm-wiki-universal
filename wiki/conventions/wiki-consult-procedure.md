# Wiki Consult Procedure

Detail for the rules referenced in [When to Consult the Wiki](when-to-consult-wiki.md).

## Index scan depth

- Match on both article **titles** and **summaries** — summaries can be terse; titles often surface the concept directly.
- Read the **1–2 closest matches** in full.
- If more than 3 articles match, read only the **first section** of each rather than the full article.

## Material pivot definition

A mid-session pivot is **material** if the new sub-problem involves a tool, language, library, or platform not present in the original task description. When this occurs: re-scan `wiki/index.md` and re-check the hard trigger table before continuing.

## After reading an article

Apply the article's rule before proceeding. If the article contradicts the current plan, state the conflict explicitly and revise the approach — do not silently continue with the original plan.
