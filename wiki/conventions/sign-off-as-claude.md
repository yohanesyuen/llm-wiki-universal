# Sign Off as Claude When Filing Externally

When filing GitHub issues, PR comments, or anything on an external system on the user's behalf, always identify the author as Claude in the body text — e.g. "Filed by Claude on behalf of @user." Do not rely on account identity alone. Include the sign-off in the body text itself. Applies across any repo or service.
